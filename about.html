About NeighborShare

Our Story.

As an U.S based sharing online platform, we, NeighborShare give local neighbors chances to share their unwanted belongings or items spend a lot of time sitting in storage unused. Since we prioritize proximity over categorization, users can see the listings that are closest to them easily, and they are able to see available items through the platform we provide. Through this resource sharing platform, people are also able to share what they need and want quickly, connecting their local communities. Users can upload a wide variety of items, with unlimited number of choices. Currently we have 5 big categories which are outdoor equipments, electronics, basic household tools, sports gears mostly for beginners, and lastly, clothings for special days.
